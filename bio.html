Business Bio

Steven M. Ledbetter, aka “Coach Stevo” is the CEO of Habitry, which he co-founded in 2014, and Betterish, co-founded in 2017. He has a BA in Philosophy from the University of Chicago and an MA in Sport Psychology from John F. Kennedy University. His area of research was using technology to facilitate habit-formation in groups. A health coach since 2008, Stevo has helped thousands of people change their health habits. In 2017, he started making the bettercast, a weekly podcast about getting better at things. Stevo also advizes early stage companies on using Motivation Science to make better products. As a writer, Stevo contributed to Dan John's Intervention in 2012, wrote We Make Communities in 2015, and wrote a weekly column for the 60mil users of MyFitnessPal from 2013 - 2015. Prior to helping people change their behavior, Stevo did marketing analytics for the blogging company Six Apart and ran the web properties of Design Within Reach.

Personal Bio

Steven M. Ledbetter, aka "Stevo", is been helping people who know what to do, but can't make themselves do it since 2008 and is an expert in the science of human behavior change. Originally from rural Georgia, he moved to San Francisco in 2005, then wised up and moved to the East Bay in 2016. He lives in an artist cooperative in Emeryville with his wife, Kat Zdan, and two italian greyhounds, one of whom is old enough to drive.
